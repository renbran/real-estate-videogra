## 💰 Cost Comparison

### Render.com (Recommended)
- **Web Service**: FREE forever ✅
- **PostgreSQL**: FREE for 90 days, then $7/mo
- **Total Year 1**: $21 (3 months free + 9 months paid)
- **Sleep**: After 15min (wakes in 30-60 sec)

**Solutions**:
- Use UptimeRobot to prevent sleep (free)
- Migrate to Supabase after 90 days (free forever)

### Fly.io (Always-On Alternative)
- **Web Service**: FREE (3 VMs) ✅
- **PostgreSQL**: FREE (3GB) ✅
- **Total**: $0/month forever
- **Sleep**: Never sleeps
- **Requires**: Credit card for verification

### Koyeb (Simplest Alternative)
- **Web Service**: FREE forever ✅
- **Database**: Use Supabase (free) ✅
- **Total**: $0/month forever
- **Sleep**: Never sleeps

## 🎯 Why Render.com?

### ✅ Advantages:
1. **No credit card required**
2. **Dashboard-based** (no CLI needed)
3. **Zero code changes** (works as-is)
4. **Auto-deploy from GitHub**
5. **PostgreSQL included**
6. **Easiest to use**
7. **15 minutes to deploy**

### ⚠️ Considerations:
1. **Sleeps after 15min** (acceptable for most projects)
   - Solution: Free UptimeRobot pinger
2. **Database costs $7/mo** after 90 days
   - Solution: Migrate to Supabase (free)

### 💡 Bottom Line:
Best balance of ease-of-use and cost. Perfect for getting started quickly.
